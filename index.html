Our Story

More Than a Meal.
It's a Feeling.

Tucked away on a quiet lane near MES College Road in Dabolim, Cafe Chai Coffee was born from a simple belief — that great food tastes even better in a beautiful place, with people you love.

We source the freshest ingredients, brew coffee with intention, and set the stage with warm lights, swaying greens, and music that lingers. Whether you're a traveller catching a breath before your flight, a student finding your favourite corner, or a family celebrating the ordinary — you belong here.
